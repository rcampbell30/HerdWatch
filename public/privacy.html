Privacy Policy

HerdWatch is a public information website about MMR vaccination coverage in England. It is designed to provide general public-interest information and does not require visitors to create accounts.

Information we collect

HerdWatch aims to collect as little personal information as possible. Standard hosting logs may record technical information such as browser type, device type, approximate location, pages visited, and time of visit.

Analytics and advertising

If analytics or advertising tools are enabled, those services may use cookies or similar technologies to understand site usage, measure performance, or serve relevant ads.

Third-party links

HerdWatch may link to public data sources or external websites. Those websites have their own privacy policies.

Contact

For privacy questions, contact the site owner through the official project channels listed on the HerdWatch repository or website.
